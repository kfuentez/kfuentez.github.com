Keeping It on the Curriculum

Sex education is so important not only to our children, but also to our growing young adults. There has long been a lack of proper sex education and instead an emphasis on abstinence. This has repeatedly shown to increase teen pregnancy and STD rates.

As a part of our goal, we would like to help our audience become educators themselves and spread the knowledge to their friends and families.

Our hope is that these references can be used by young adults, campus advisors, concerned friends, or just anyone seeking to gain and share this information.
