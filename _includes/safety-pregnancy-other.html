Plans B, C, and D

So you messed up. Maybe your birth control method didn't work, or maybe you forgot. It's not the end of the world. Here are some options to keep in mind.

Emergency Contraceptives
Many of us have woken up one morning realizing we didn't use a condom the night before. It's okay. Luckily, Plan B is super easy to get from a pharmacy. Of course, there are other types you can read about here.
Abortion
Abortions are a lot more common than you may think. Actually, about 3 in 10 women have had one. We aren't encouraging you to get an abortion, but we want you to know it's not wrong. Your body is your property, and you should be in charge of it.
