What, when, and how

Half of all sexually active people will contract and STD by age 25, and the majority of these people get them from having sex without a condom.

STDs have a very negative connotation. People often associate them with whores or sluts, or that frat guy in your math class. The truth is, anyone has some risk of getting an infection or disease, even if it's your first time having sex, and even if you use a condom.
