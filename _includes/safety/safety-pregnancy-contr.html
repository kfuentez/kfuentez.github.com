Preventing Reproduction

We're no doctors. We can't tell you which birth control is best for you, but we can give you a small run down of a few popular ones. (click for a description)

implant
A small, thin plastic implant that's inserted in the upper arm.
Pros:
prevents pregnancy for up to 4 years
you don't have to remember to take a pill everyday
Cons:
may cause irregular bleeding
patch
Just a small square that you stick on your skin, kind of like a band-aid.
Pros:
convenient weekly switch-out
no prescription needed
Cons:
may cause irregular bleeding
pill
Just a good ol' pill you pop in daily.
Pros:
easy to attain from the doctor
no type of insertion procedure
Cons:
can be easily forgotten
prescription is needed
shot
A shot you get at the doctor's every few months.
Pros:
lasts about 3 months
quick and easy procedure
Cons:
regular doctor visits

These are just few of the many kinds of the contraceptives out there. For more details on different kinds, check out this site.
